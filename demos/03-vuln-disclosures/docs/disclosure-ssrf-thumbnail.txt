---
title: Coordinated Disclosure - SSRF in Thumbnail Fetcher
date: 2026-03-09
source: Cognis Digital AppSec Disclosures
tags: appsec, ssrf, network
---

A server-side request forgery issue was reported in a thumbnail-generation
service. The service fetched an image from a user-supplied URL without
restricting the destination, allowing requests to internal addresses and the
cloud metadata endpoint.

Impact: an attacker could induce the server to make requests to internal
services that are not reachable from the public internet.

Remediation: validate the destination against an allowlist of public image
hosts, deny requests to private and link-local address ranges, and disable
automatic redirect following. The fix shipped with a unit test covering the
private-range denial. Documented under coordinated, authorized disclosure.
