# Advisory on Grid Resilience During Peak Demand

Operators of regional transmission networks should review contingency plans
ahead of sustained peak-demand windows. When generation reserves fall below the
documented margin, controlled load curtailment is preferable to uncontrolled
cascading outages. This advisory summarizes defensive measures only.

Key recommendations:

- Verify that backup substations have current firmware and tested failover.
- Stage portable generation near critical loads such as water treatment.
- Coordinate demand response with large industrial consumers in advance.

The analysis is descriptive and intended to support planning and resilience.
