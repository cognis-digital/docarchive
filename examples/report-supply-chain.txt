Critical minerals underpin modern manufacturing, from batteries to magnets.
Concentrated processing capacity creates supply-chain fragility: a single
disruption can ripple across downstream industries for months.

This report reviews diversification strategies. Recycling recovers material
from end-of-life products and reduces dependence on primary extraction.
Stockpiling smooths short-term shocks but ties up capital. Long-term supply
security depends on transparent reporting and diversified sourcing.

Nothing here constitutes investment advice; the overview is analytical.
